FAQs

Frequently asked question

01. What is Devops as a Service(DaaS)?

DevOps as a Service (DaaS) is a managed service that provides companies with the tools, expertise, and infrastructure to implement and maintain a DevOps culture and workflow. This allows them to automate processes, streamline development and deployment cycles, and ultimately deliver applications faster and more reliably.

02. What are the benefits of using DaaS?

There are several benefits to using DaaS, including:
Faster deployments: DaaS can help automate manual tasks and streamline workflows, leading to faster deployments and quicker time to market.
Improved quality: DaaS can help ensure consistent quality by automating testing and deployment processes.
Increased efficiency: DaaS can free up your internal IT staff to focus on more strategic initiatives.
Reduced costs: DaaS can help you avoid the upfront costs of investing in DevOps tools and infrastructure.

03. Will you integrate with my existing tools and infrastructure?

We can integrate with a wide range of existing tools and infrastructure. We will work with you to assess your current environment and develop a plan for seamless integration.

04. Can I try the service before subscribing?

We offer a 7-day free trial so you can explore our DevOps services firsthand. See the quality and speed of our work by submitting your own requests during the trial.

05. Do you offer custom solutions?

We understand every business has unique needs. We do offer custom solutions to tackle your specific challenges, from automation to security.
